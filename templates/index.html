Welcome to Mount Prospect Child Care Center

“Learning through play - play with a purpose is the work of young children.”

Mt. Prospect Child Care Center offers a progressive, well-equipped center, featuring full and part-time programs for children 15 months through 10 years.

Our center provides separate, age specific programs and supervised indoor and outdoor activities. We have a fully enclosed, on-site playground, with state of the art safety grass.  Children are taught both academic and interpersonal skills, confidence, and social awareness with emphasis on learning through play.
