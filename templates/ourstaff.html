Our Staff

Ms. Anne Marie has been working at Mt Prospect Childcare Center for 5 years. She typically works with the older pre-school children, as well as the kindergarteners who attend for before or after school care. She has an early childhood degree and has worked with special needs children, as well as in a Headstart program. She spent 7 years as a Girl Scout leader. In her free time, she enjoys gardening, cooking, baking, and attending concerts.

Ms. Tracy began working at Mt Prospect Childcare Center in 1986. In 1994, she took a break to stay home with her 3 daughters, and returned to the center in 2006. Ms Tracy is the pre-school teacher for the 3s and young 4s class. She has an Associates Degree in child development. In her free time, she enjoys spending time with her dog Molly, taking vacations with her family, and playing cards with friends.

Ms. Carol has been a teacher at Mt Prospect Childcare Center for 28 years! She typically works with the youngest group of children (15 months to 2 years). She has two sons and two granddaughters whom she loves to spend time with. In her free time, she loves to garden.

Ms. Katie started working at Mt Prospect Childcare last year, upon completion of her High School's "preschool program," which included 4 years of child development. She works most often with the 2s and young 3s. She loves sports, and currently coaches a community cheerleading team in her free time. She also loves to cook and work on cars!

Ms. Lindsey has been with Mt Prospect Childcare since the beginning of the 2011/2012 school year.
